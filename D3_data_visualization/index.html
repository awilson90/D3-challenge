Myth or Truth: Are wealthier people healthier than the rest of us?

According to a study by Jr. Data Analyst Arturo Wilson, there is enough evident to suggest that significant relationship between level of poverty and access to healthcare. Using data provided by the United States Census Bureau, Wilson produced a chart to illustrate percentage of poverty in different states vs. percentage of lack of access to healthcare.

The relationship between these two factors is close to linear. The only notable exception seems to be Texas which compared to North Carolina or comparable states like California, Florida, or South Carolina, the expected outcome does not occur.
